IS 310: Digital Objects Showcase 📚🍽️

We are Group 4! Welcome to our group's digital objects collection for IS310! This showcase features individual explorations of cultural objects from our research on Mass Digitization & Digital Libraries.

Each of us has selected and analyzed a food-related digitized cultural object, examining how it's been documented, preserved, and presented in digital archives!
