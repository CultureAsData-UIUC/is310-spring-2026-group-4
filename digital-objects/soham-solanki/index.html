Soham Solanki's Digital Object Project Food and the Digital Archive

Food and the Digital Archive

Metadata

Object Type: Digital Archive

Focus Area: Food and Culinary Culture

Key Themes: Preservation, Accessibility, Representation

Time Period: 2000s-Present

Location: Global

Description

This project explores how food-related cultural objects are preserved and presented in digital archives. It examines the challenges and opportunities of digitizing culinary heritage, including issues of accessibility, representation, and cultural sensitivity. The project also highlights examples of digital archives that focus on food culture and analyzes their impact on public engagement and cultural preservation.

Key Transformations

  • Shift from physical to digital preservation of culinary heritage
  • Increased accessibility of food-related cultural objects through online platforms
  • Challenges of representing diverse food cultures in digital archives
  • Impact of digital archives on public engagement with food culture
