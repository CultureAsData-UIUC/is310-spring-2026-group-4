The Mouquin Restaurant and Wine Co. (1920)

Object Overview

This object is a digitized 1920 restaurant menu from The Mouquin Restaurant and Wine Co., preserved by the New York Public Library. It is part of The Buttolph Collection of Menus, a large archive that documents restaurant culture and food history in the United States.

Metadata

Title: The Mouquin Restaurant and Wine Co.

Date Created: 1920

Collection: The Buttolph Collection of Menus

Library Division: Rare Book Division, NYPL

Physical Description: 35.5 x 38 cm

Language: English

Note: Menu is falling apart (physical deterioration)

Description

Restaurant menus function as cultural artifacts because they record pricing, food trends, dining norms, and branding practices of a specific historical moment/time. This 1920 menu reflects early twentieth-century urban restaurant culture and demonstrates how everyday objects like menus become historical records.

Why It Matters

Through digitization, this fragile menu is preserved and made accessible without requiring physical handling. While the scan captures the visual layout, typography, and structural design, it cant really fully reproduce the texture, weight, or physical wear of the original object.
